Wendao Changsheng audio notice
==============================

Runtime audio is described by audio/audio_manifest_v2.json. Third-party files
keep their original licenses and are not relicensed by the project's AGPL
notice. The release bundle includes the corresponding full notices in
audio/licenses/.

Third-party sources in the curated v2 set:

* 0 A.D. music by Wildfire Games: Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 3.0
  (CC BY-SA 3.0). See audio/licenses/0ad-audio-LICENSE.txt.
* Kenney Interface Sounds, RPG Audio, Impact Sounds, Digital Audio, Sci-Fi
  Sounds and Music Jingles: Creative Commons Zero (CC0). See
  audio/licenses/kenney-cc0.txt. These provide separate UI, blade impact,
  shield, spell, recovery, status, phase-change and outcome source families.
* Loopable Dungeon Ambience by JaggedStone, published on OpenGameArt: CC0. See
  audio/licenses/opengameart-dungeon-ambient-cc0.txt.
* Views From Atop the Jade King's Throne by HitCtrl, published on OpenGameArt:
  CC BY 3.0. See audio/licenses/opengameart-jade-throne-ccby3.txt.

The manifest records a pinned official archive URL and archive SHA-256, exact
member path, source SHA-256, runtime SHA-256, creator, license URL,
attribution, processing steps, measured audio properties and runtime path for
every Kenney asset. Runtime SFX are byte-identical published Ogg files; event
gain is applied non-destructively by AudioDirector. MP3 files, archives and
temporary source directories are never runtime inputs.

The retired procedural audio set and v1 manifest were removed from the product
source tree. AudioDirector resolves only the curated v2 manifest; no legacy
runtime audio is shipped or used as a fallback.
